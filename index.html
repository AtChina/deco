Manage Your Storage Accounts like a Pro

Project Deco: A file explorer for your Azure Blob Storage accounts, enabling you to easily work with your assets and containers from any platform. Create and delete containers, upload, download, and delete whole folders and files, preview media assets - with the free Azure Storage Explorer, you're in full control of your assets.
